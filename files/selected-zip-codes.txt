Selected US Zip Codes 
=====================

Zip codes and other information of cities in the US.

Columns

* 0: Zip code
* 1: Latitude
* 2: Longitude
* 3: City
* 4: State
* 5: County

Original file dowloaded from <https://www.gaslampmedia.com/download-zip-code-latitude-longitude-city-state-county-csv/> on 2017-09-10.

Eeements selected with `grep '^"[0-9][0-9]123"'`.
